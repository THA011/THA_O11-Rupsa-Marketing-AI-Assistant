About the platform

Built for the work that happens before and after a campaign is designed.

RAIP is the working direction for THA_O11. RUPSA SACCO is the first profile, and the goal is to keep the platform reusable for other organizations later.

The idea

A good campaign starts before the design file. RAIP keeps the goal, audience, channel, offer, and evidence in one place so the creative work has a clear reason behind it.

The platform is organized around brand profiles, campaign briefs, local previews, scoring notes, and eventually research reporting.

Why it matters

SACCO communication needs trust and clarity. A campaign can look good and still fail if the offer is vague, the audience is wrong, or nobody records what happened after publishing.

Principles

Simple enough for daily use. Strong enough for research.

Brand first

Every output should respect the selected organization's identity, tone, rules, and approval flow.

Evidence first

Campaigns should be scored, tested, compared, and improved using statistical thinking.

Reusable by design

RUPSA is the first profile. Later organizations should use the same platform with new profile data.
