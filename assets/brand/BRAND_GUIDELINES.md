## Typography

### Fonts

**Primary Font:** Inter  
- Headings (H1–H3): Bold, 700–900 weight
- Body Text: Regular, 400 weight
- Buttons: Semibold, 600 weight

**Fallback Stack:** Inter, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif

### Sizing

```
H1: 48px | Line Height: 56px | Weight: 700
H2: 36px | Line Height: 44px | Weight: 700
H3: 24px | Line Height: 32px | Weight: 600
Body: 16px | Line Height: 24px | Weight: 400
Caption: 12px | Line Height: 16px | Weight: 500
```

---

## Logo Usage

### Logo Proportions
- Keep original 16:9 aspect ratio
- Minimum size: 100px width
- Leave 20px minimum white space around logo
- Never distort or rotate

### Logo Variations
1. **Full Logo** — Logo + "THA_O11" text
2. **Mark Only** — Logo symbol only
3. **Horizontal** — Logo on left, text on right
4. **Vertical** — Logo above text

## Color Usage in Materials

### Dos ✅
- Use lime green (#00FF00) for CTAs
- Black for main text, headings
- White backgrounds for readability
- Thin green/black lines as dividers
- High contrast for accessibility

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use pale green (use pure #00FF00)
- Don't mix multiple accent colors
- Don't fade logo colors
- Don't use green text on white (low contrast)
- Don't distort logo proportions
