# RAIP Product Roadmap

## Phase 1 - RUPSA Brand-Aware Marketing AI

Goal: Build the first working assistant around RUPSA SACCO.

Deliverables:

- RUPSA brand profile.
- Campaign brief schema.
- Audience/persona list for SACCO marketing.
- Prompt templates for posters, social posts, WhatsApp, SMS, email, and radio scripts.
- Basic campaign scoring.
- Manual approval workflow.

Success measure:

- A user can create a campaign brief and receive usable, brand-consistent campaign outputs.

## Phase 2 - Analytics and Statistical Decision Support

Goal: Make campaign decisions measurable.

Deliverables:

- A/B test planning.
- Campaign KPI tracking.
- Engagement and conversion estimates.
- Regression and logistic regression workflows.
- Customer segmentation with clustering.
- Confidence intervals and simple statistical explanations.
- Dashboard for performance and recommendations.

Success measure:

- A user can compare campaign variants and explain why one is expected to perform better.

## Phase 3 - Research and Academic Reporting

Goal: Turn platform data into research-grade outputs.

Deliverables:

- Survey builder.
- Data cleaning workflow.
- APA-style report generator.
- Literature review and methodology support.
- Export to CSV, Excel, SPSS-friendly formats, and PDF.
- Thesis/capstone documentation templates.

Success measure:

- The platform can support a statistics bachelor's project using real marketing and survey data.

## Phase 4 - Multi-Organization SaaS

Goal: Move from RUPSA-only to configurable clients.

Deliverables:

- Brand profile manager.
- Organization/user roles.
- Client-specific assets and templates.
- Approval workflows per organization.
- Isolated campaign and analytics data.
- Billing/subscription-ready architecture.

Success measure:

- A second organization can use the platform without changing core code.

## Phase 5 - Agent Team and Automation

Goal: Add specialist AI agents and channel integrations.

Deliverables:

- Marketing Director AI.
- Copywriter AI.
- Graphic Designer AI.
- Video Producer AI.
- Statistician AI.
- Research Assistant AI.
- Prompt Engineer AI.
- Social media, WhatsApp, email, and SMS automation.

Success measure:

- The system can plan, produce, score, approve, publish, and analyze campaigns with minimal manual switching between tools.
